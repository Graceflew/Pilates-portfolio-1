pilates

why choose pilates?

Pilates is a discipline that concentrates in improving flexibility, builds strength, develops control and endurance in the entire body. The exercises emphasis is on controlled movements and alignment. Along with a breathing pattern, pilates enthusiasts will develop a strong core, improving coordination and balance.

Core Strength

Pilates focuses on working and strengthening your core – abdominal, back, pelvic floor and glutes muscles – you’ll find that one of the benefits of Pilates is that you will develop more controlled movements and slimmer stomach.
